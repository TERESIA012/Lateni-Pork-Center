OUR STORY

Lateni Pork Centre

History

Lateni Pork Centre was started a month ago after a team
of four members discovered that there was scarcity of pork in Nairobi County.
The team has created a website that links the farmers and consumers to
increase availability of Fresh Pork in Nairobi County.
name was derived from the first two letters on the Members' names.
members are Langat,Teresia,Nicodemus and Nick (LATENI.)

Mission

To provide our customers with high-quality produced pork
while building a sustainable business to support the regional pork industry.

Vision

To be the hub of the Nairobi Pork Industry,
relationships to provide excellent pork products to
our families and yours,upholding the highest standards of health and service.

Values

  • Honesty
  • Fairness
  • Team-Work
  • Safety
